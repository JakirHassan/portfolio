# Onboarding Conversion Funnel Overhaul

## Executive Summary
Our registration to onboarding setup completion had a massive drop-off of **42%** during the payment gateway onboarding phase. By mapping user flows and conducting user testing, we identified critical friction points and ran structured experiments.

## Objectives
- Improve registration-to-activation conversion by >20%.
- Reduce Time-to-Value (TTV) for new accounts.
- Maintain security standards.

## The Friction Points
1. **Information Overload**: Requesting bank details before users understood the value proposition.
2. **Lack of Guidance**: No progress indicators during a multi-step setup.

## Solutions Implemented
- **Deferred Setup**: Users can explore the dashboard with sandboxed mock data before providing banking credentials.
- **Progress Tracker**: Added a clear interactive tracker showing completed tasks.

## Results
- Shipped 4 core A/B experiments.
- Boosted overall signup completion by **28%**.
- TTV dropped from 12 minutes to under **3 minutes**.
